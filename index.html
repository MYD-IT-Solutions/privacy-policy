Introduction

Welcome to iTutor Ethiopia! This privacy policy outlines how iTutor Ethiopia, owned by MYD IT Solutions PLC, collects, uses, shares, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

1. Information Collection and Use

iTutor Ethiopia collects information from users through our mobile application and web platform. We are the sole owners of the information collected and will not sell, share, or rent it to others. The information collected includes: Contact Information: When registering for our services, users are required to provide their name and email address. This information is used to contact users about relevant topics and to assist with password retrieval

1.1 Mobile Application Registration

In order to use some features of this application, users must first complete the registration form. During registration, users are required to give their contact information (name and e-mail address). This information is used to contact users about the topics on our mobile application for which they have expressed interest and to enable users to retrieve lost passwords.

2. Web Statistics

We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the mobile application, and gather demographic information for reporting purposes. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

3. Sharing

We may share aggregated demographic information with partners, sponsors, and donors to secure funding for continued service provision. This information does not identify individual users.

4. Links

This mobile application contains links to other mobile applications. Please be aware that iTutor Ethiopia does not claim any responsibility for the privacy practices of such other mobile applications. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our mobile application and to read the privacy statements of each and every mobile application that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this mobile application.

5. Security

This mobile application takes every precaution to protect users' information, and we have security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information under our control.

6. Surveys

Occasionally, our mobile application requests information from users via web surveys. Participation in these surveys or contests is completely voluntary and the user therefore has a choice whether or not to disclose this information. Information requested may include demographic data (such as zip code and age), but nothing that can identify any individual person will be collected. Survey results will be used for purposes of monitoring or improving the use and satisfaction of this mobile application.

7. Updates

We may also send the user mobile application and service announcement updates. Members are not able to unsubscribe from service announcements that contain important information about the service. We communicate with users to provide requested services and to discuss issues relating to their accounts via e-mail or phone.

8. Correction/Updating Personal Information

If a user's personally identifiable information changes (such as name or e-mail address), or if a user no longer desires our service, we will endeavor to provide a way to correct, update, or remove that the personal data that was provided to us. Users can use the "My Personal Info" link from their homepage or they can e-mail our Help Desk to change, correct, or remove information.

9. Choice/Opt-in

Our users are given the opportunity to "opt in" to newsletters and mobile application update e-mail messages. To unsubscribe from mailings, please see that section above. To unsubscribe from any updates, users can use the "My Personal Info" link from their homepage. To avoid receiving certain topic updates, the user can simply deselect the topics and select the "Update" button.

10. Notification of Changes

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our home page so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users by e-mail. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected.
